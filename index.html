DRAFTTRAINER

Drafttrainer is a sophisticated Magic the Gathering learning tool, where you can draft alongside bots developed using machine-learning and trained on successful 7-win drafts on MTG Arena. The result is an experience that more closely resembles playing against experienced human players. Our pick orders are also generated using a complex statistical analysis of draft data. Drafttrainer is about emphasizing a data-driven approach to improving at magic the gathering.
